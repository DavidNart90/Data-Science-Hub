What isData Science?

Data science is the study of extracting useful insights from noisy, structured, and unstructured data using scientific methodologies, programming skills, and understanding of mathematics and statistics. In the following sections, we'll go through some of the major fields of data science, which will help you narrow down your options when deciding on a speciality.

Data Mining

Data mining, also known as knowledge discovery in data (KDD), is the process of uncovering patterns and other valuable information from large data sets. Given the evolution of data warehousing technology and the growth of big data, adoption of data mining techniques has rapidly accelerated over the last couple of decades, assisting companies by transforming their raw data into useful knowledge. However, despite the fact that that technology continuously evolves to handle data at a large-scale, leaders still face challenges with scalability and automation.

Data Visualization

Data Visualization is a prominent part of Data Science that deals with analyzing data using graphical representations and software that involves the usage of programming languages such as Python. Visualization softwares such as Power BI, Excel, Tableau, and Looker use statistics to communicate information clearly and efficiently.
